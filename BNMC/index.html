WELCOME TO BADRI NARAYAN MUKTESHWAR COLLEGE

Badri Narayan Mukteshwar College, Barhiya is the oldest institute of higher learning in the Lakhisarai district. Late Badri Narayan Mukteshwar had a dream to open a higher learning institute for the students of rural area. B.N.M College, Barhiya believes in providing quality education and information about courses to students & their parents to match their needs in collaboration of Search Education Portal.

The College ensures publicity and transparency in the admission process. It is very particular to cater to the needs of differently abled students. The college is primitive to gender sensitivity, inclusive education and environment friendly ethos among students. The Purpose of the B.N.M.College, Barhiya is to stimulate and coordinate the institutional resources of the College and to challenge faculties and students.

PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

It’s a pleasure to be a professor in charge and Principal of an educational institute of a very remote sensitive area BADRI NARAYAN MUKTESHWAR COLLEGE BARHIYA. A dream come true of the visionary Late Badri Narayan to enhance the quality of higher education of the rural society, particularly of the traditional girls/women of this remote area. One of the oldest higher educational institutions of old Munger District

Any young person taking admission in this premier institution must not only have the required marks, but must aim at excellence in whatever he or she attempts, all the time keeping in mind and in practice, a disciplined and a diligent attitude. I welcome the prospective students of B.N.M College to its campus, to benefit from its rich academic tradition so that they may step out into the world as worthy citizens of our country.

Dr. Ashutosh Kumar

Principal, BNM College
